This project is aimed at analyzing and attempting to predict future housing market conditions within the state of Nebraska over the next 5 year period. It additionally looks at the buying power and sustainability of said market with the current and projected trends in wages within the state. Using the SARIMAX modeling technique the analysis attempts to build out the next 5 years of data based on the last 3 years of housing market data. Based on the findings from this model the analysis goes on to describe issues within the current framework that could contributing to the results we are seeing and attempts to give suggestions that may aid in resolving the conflict shown.
